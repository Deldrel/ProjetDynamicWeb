We are two friends who are passionate about comics. In 2020, during the period of COVID19 and its famous confinement, we realized how important comics were in our lives. As time went on, we realized that people were reading more and more comics, so we decided to market our collection. This collection includes all kinds of original comics, because before belonging to us, it belonged to our grandparents, then to our parents.
Later on, we also bought other comics and opened our store in 2021. This one works so well, that afterwards, this year 2022 is the right one to launch us in a new type of business: The online purchase.
